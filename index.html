How it works

From brief to results in four steps

Whether you're evaluating candidates, assessing skills for a promotion, or running internal certification — Elora IQ handles the entire workflow from creation to final report.

Step 01
Describe your assessment
Chat with Elora IQ's AI builder. Tell it the purpose — a hiring test, a promotion review, a skills certification — and it creates a complete, tailored assessment in seconds.
Step 02
Share the link
Publish and share a public or private link. Invite individuals by email directly from the platform — each gets a unique access link tied to their identity.
Step 03
Monitor in real time
While the assessment is in progress, Elora IQ monitors for tab switching, copy-paste, AI usage, and plagiarism — alerting participants and logging every integrity event.
Step 04
Receive the report
The moment someone submits, AI marks every answer and emails you a full report — scores, integrity analysis, and a marking breakdown per question. Participants see nothing.

Features

Everything you need to assess smarter

From AI-powered creation to live proctoring and instant results — every feature is designed to make assessments faster, fairer, and more reliable.

Conversational AI creator
Describe what you need in plain English. Elora IQ asks one question at a time and builds the full assessment — MCQ, descriptive, coding challenges, and soft skills.
Smart time budgeting
As you add questions, the AI tracks your time budget in real time and alerts you if selections exceed the limit — with instant adjustment options.
Live proctoring
Six integrity signals monitored simultaneously — tab switches, copy-paste attempts, AI usage detection, time off-tab, plagiarism, and real-time candidate alerts.
AI marking engine
MCQ marked instantly. Descriptive and coding answers scored by AI against the rubric generated during creation. Custom questions flagged as Suggestive marks.
Automated reports
The moment someone submits, you receive a full report by email — scores, integrity percentage, event timeline, and AI marking breakdown per question.
Private invite links
Send personalised assessment links to specific people by email. Each person gets a unique URL — no shared access, no results leaked through forwarded links.
