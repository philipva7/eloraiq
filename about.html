About Elora IQ

Built to assess people the right way

Elora IQ was created because assessments should be intelligent, fair, and useful — whether you're hiring new talent, recognising internal growth, or certifying skills across your organisation.

Our story

Assessments are one of the most important decisions an organisation makes — yet most of them are created manually, marked inconsistently, and used only for hiring.

We built Elora IQ to change that. The idea started from a simple frustration: building a proper technical assessment from scratch takes hours, marking descriptive answers is subjective, and nobody has a good system for running the same quality of assessment internally as they do for hiring.

With the arrival of powerful AI models, we saw an opportunity to change all of that. Elora IQ uses AI to generate tailored assessments in minutes, mark every type of answer with consistency and accuracy, and deliver results instantly — while keeping candidates honest with live proctoring and integrity monitoring.

But we designed it to go beyond hiring. A great engineer deserves to be promoted on the basis of a fair skills assessment, not just a manager's opinion. A compliance team should be able to certify 200 employees with the same rigour as hiring 2. Elora IQ is built for all of it.

"Every person deserves to be assessed fairly.
Every organisation deserves results they can trust."

That's the belief Elora IQ was built on.

Who Elora IQ is for

Elora IQ is designed for any organisation that needs to assess people — fairly, efficiently, and at scale. Here's where teams are using it:

Talent acquisition
Screen and assess candidates before interviews. Build role-specific tests in minutes, share a link, and receive AI-marked reports the moment each candidate submits.
Internal promotions
Make promotion decisions on evidence, not opinion. Assess whether someone is ready for a senior role with a structured, consistent test — the same way you'd assess an external hire.
Skills certification
Run periodic skill assessments across teams. Certify that your engineers, analysts, or support staff meet the standards their roles require — with automated, consistent marking.
Compliance testing
Certify that staff have understood and can apply compliance requirements. Run assessments for entire departments and receive instant reports without any manual review.

What we believe

Elora IQ is built around a set of principles that shape every decision we make — from how the AI marks answers to how we display results.

01
Fairness above all
Every candidate or employee should be assessed on the same criteria, with the same consistency. AI removes the subjectivity that humans bring to marking.
02
Integrity is non-negotiable
Results are only useful if they're honest. Our live proctoring and integrity scoring give organisations confidence that results reflect genuine ability.
03
Speed without compromise
A great assessment shouldn't take days to build. Elora IQ creates professional, tailored assessments in minutes — without cutting corners on quality.
04
Insight for decision makers
Results go to the person making the decision — not the person being assessed. Detailed reports give organisations everything they need to make the right call.
05
Built to scale
Whether you're assessing 2 people or 2,000, Elora IQ delivers the same quality of experience. No extra setup, no extra cost per question.
06
Privacy by design
Candidates never see their scores or reports. Results belong to the organisation running the assessment — always, by default.

The team

Elora IQ is an early-stage product being built by a small, focused team passionate about making intelligent assessments accessible to every organisation — not just the ones with HR departments large enough to build them manually.

We come from backgrounds in product, engineering, and people operations — which means we've sat on both sides of the assessment table. We've spent hours building tests from scratch, marked hundreds of answers by hand, and made hiring and promotion decisions with far less information than we should have had.

That experience shapes everything we build. Every feature in Elora IQ exists because we felt the pain of not having it. The conversational creator exists because writing questions from scratch is slow and inconsistent. The integrity scoring exists because we've seen how hard it is to trust results without visibility into how candidates behaved. The automated reports exist because decisions shouldn't wait for someone to find time to compile a spreadsheet.

We're building Elora IQ in public — shaped by early users, improved with every assessment run through it, and committed to staying genuinely useful rather than just impressive. If you have feedback, ideas, or just want to tell us what's broken, we want to hear it.
